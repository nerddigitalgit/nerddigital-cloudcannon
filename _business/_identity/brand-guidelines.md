## 6. Visual Identity: "Industrial Editorial" System

**Visual Metaphor:** The Architect's Desk
**Core Vibe:** Premium Utility

*We are not "marketing" at you; we are showing you the blueprints.*

### Color Palette: "Paper, Ink, and Laser"

Moving away from "Dark Mode SaaS" (generic) and "Bright White Marketing" (cheap). The base is warm and editorial.

#### The Base (Structure)

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Paper White** | `#F9F9F7` | Backgrounds - subtle warm grey/cream |
| **Engineering Black** | `#111111` | Text/Lines - deep charcoal, not pure black |
| **Technical Grey** | `#E5E5E5` | Borders/Dividers |

#### The Signals (Action)

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Signal Green** | `#00C853` | Status/Success - "Systems Operational," success metrics |
| **Laser Pink** | `#FF0080` | Primary Action/Focus - buttons, annotations, "look here" moments |

#### The Secondary Palette (Functional UI)

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Signal Green Tint** | `#E6FAF0` | Success backgrounds, positive states, subtle green highlights |
| **Laser Pink Tint** | `#FFE6F2` | Focus backgrounds, notification badges, subtle pink highlights |

#### The Tertiary Palette (Structure Expansion)

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Charcoal** | `#222222` | Dark backgrounds, footer, contrast sections |
| **Slate Grey** | `#666666` | Secondary text, muted labels, captions |
| **Structure Grey** | `#CCCCCC` | Disabled states, subtle borders, background accents |

#### The Golden Rule
**Never use Pink and Green as background gradients. Use them like ink stamps on a piece of paper.**

### Typography: "Smart & Technical"

| Element | Font Type | Examples | Why |
|---------|-----------|----------|-----|
| **Headlines (H1, H2)** | Serif | Fraunces, Playfair Display | Signals "Authority" and "Magazine Quality" |
| **Body & UI** | Sans-Serif / Mono | Inter, Geist, Roboto Mono | Reads like a manual. High legibility. |

### UI Components

#### Buttons
- **Shape:** Sharp corners or very tight radius (2px)
- **Primary:** Solid Pink with White text
- **Secondary:** Thin Black Outline with Black text

#### Containers/Cards
- White background on `#F9F9F7` page background
- Thin 1px grey border
- Zero or very subtle shadow - "flat print" feel

#### Lead Magnets
- **Do not display as "3D Books." Display as Files.**
- Rectangular, thin border, clear title
- Looks like a dossier

### Imagery: "The Markup" Filter

1. **The Source:** Take a client photo or screenshot
2. **The Desaturation:** Lower saturation by 20-30% (slightly matte)
3. **The Annotation:** Apply Pink hand-drawn circle or arrow over key element
4. **The Context:** Place on clean grid background

**Result:** Looks like the expert is analyzing data. Turns a "testimonial" into a "case study."

### Layout Principles

- **Grid Visible:** Use faint grey lines (like graph paper) - emphasizes "Engineering"
- **High Whitespace:** If you think there's too much space, add more. Luxury = space.
- **Asymmetry:** Don't center everything. Align text left (technical standard).

Direct, warm, no-BS, strategic, nerdy-but-accessible

- Short sentences. Punchy.
- Max 3-line paragraphs
