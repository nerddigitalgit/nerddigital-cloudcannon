# CLAUDE.md

## Project: The Expert Conversion Code

5-day email course (lead magnet) for Nerd Digital.

## Quick Context

**Course Name:** The Expert Conversion Code

**Subtitle:** 5 days to convert more leads into high-ticket clients

**Target:** Experts struggling to convert leads into $5K+ clients

**Style:** Kallaway-style (teach one thing, get out, soft P.S. pitch)

## The 5 Frameworks

| Day | Framework | One-liner |
|-----|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | Trust Bridge | Give value before you pitch |
| 2 | Premium Positioning Sentence | Get specific to charge premium |
| 3 | Brand Your Ideas | Create your own frameworks |
| 4 | Open Water Strategy | Market to the 97%, not just the 3% |
| 5 | Mini-Launch Method | Test the click before you build |

## Files

- `THE-EXPERT-CONVERSION-CODE.md` — **Complete email sequence** (use this)
- `PROJECT-BRIEF.md` — Project context and structure

## Email Style

- ONE concept per email
- Teach it, demonstrate it, get out
- Soft P.S. pitch to book a call
- No homework or action steps
- Short paragraphs, 4th-grade reading level

## What NOT to Include

- Trust theory / academic frameworks
- "Mistakes" framing (negative)
- 10-minute action steps
- Heavy, long-winded content
- Guru-speak
