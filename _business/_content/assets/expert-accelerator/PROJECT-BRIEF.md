# PROJECT BRIEF: The Expert Conversion Code

## Overview

**Course Name:** The Expert Conversion Code

**Subtitle:** 5 days to convert more leads into high-ticket clients

**Format:** 6 emails (Day 0-5), one concept per email, Kallaway-style (teach and get out)

---

## Target Audience

Experts who sell their knowledge:
- Coaches
- Consultants
- Course creators
- Service providers

They're good at what they do but struggle to convert leads into high-ticket ($5K+) clients. They're either pitching too early, sounding too generic, or fishing in the same crowded puddle as everyone else.

---

## The 5 Frameworks

| Day | Framework | What It Teaches |
|-----|-----------|-----------------|
| 1 | Trust Bridge | Give value before you pitch |
| 2 | Premium Positioning Sentence | Get specific to charge premium |
| 3 | Brand Your Ideas | Create your own frameworks |
| 4 | Open Water Strategy | Market to the 97%, not just the 3% |
| 5 | Mini-Launch Method | Test the click before you build |

---

## Email Structure (Kallaway Style)

Each email:
- Teaches ONE concept
- Shows why it matters for conversion
- Gets out fast
- Soft P.S. pitch to book a call
- No homework. No action steps.
