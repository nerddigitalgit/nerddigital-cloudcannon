
## The Expert Conversion Code (Lead Magnet)

**What it is:** Free 5-day email course on converting leads into high-ticket clients.

**Subtitle:** 5 days to convert more leads into high-ticket clients

**Target audience:** Coaches, consultants, course creators, service providers who are good at what they do but struggle to convert leads into $5K+ clients.

**The 5 Frameworks:**
1. **Trust Bridge** - Give value before you pitch
2. **Premium Positioning Sentence** - Get specific to charge premium
3. **Brand Your Ideas** - Create your own frameworks
4. **Open Water Strategy** - Market to the 97%, not just the 3%
5. **Mini-Launch Method** - Test the click before you build

**Course Structure:**
- Day 0: Welcome - What's coming
- Day 1: Trust Bridge - Why leads ghost your pitch
- Day 2: Premium Positioning Sentence - Why you can't charge what you're worth
- Day 3: Brand Your Ideas - How to create content that makes you unforgettable
- Day 4: Open Water Strategy - Why you're fishing in a puddle
- Day 5: Mini-Launch Method - A simple test to get 10x more leads

**Style:** Kallaway-style. Teach one thing, get out, soft P.S. pitch. No homework. No action steps. 4th-grade reading level, short punchy sentences.
